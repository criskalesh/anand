Welcome to Anand Beach Resorts.

Anand Beach Resort is an ideal place to enjoy the fullness of the beauty of Kovalam, the enchanting beach destination. Located at Chowara Beach on a hillock 9 km south of the famous Kovalam Beach, Trivandrum, Kerala.


The resort along with a pristine beach and well laid out Villas result in the perfect environment for your Ayurveda and Yoga experience. Most of all it is our team at Anand that ensures your complete rejuvenation by providing a traditional Ayurvedic treatment with the utmost personalized care. The drive from the airport is about 30 minutes by taxi - and as you pass through the gates, the beauty of nature and beach begins.


Our goal is to provide this exceptional experience by bringing together the grace of nature and the healing touches of a traditional Ayurvedic Treatment. Enter the world of holistic healing and let Ayurveda and meditation restore your physical, mental, spiritual and emotional equilibrium.

Local Attractions


Popular with tourists since the 1930s, Kovalam Beach is only a 10-minute ‘tuk-tuk’ ride away from Resort and is a great place to pick up supplies. a ‘tuk-tuk’ is basically a motorbike with a shell on it’s back for passengers to ride in, making it a fun, breezy and inexpensive way to get around.
